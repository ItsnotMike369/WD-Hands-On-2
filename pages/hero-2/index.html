STRANGER THINGS

Drama · Fantasy · Horror

2016 - Present 4 Seasons TV Series

In a small town where everyone knows everyone, a peculiar incident starts a chain of events that leads to a child's disappearance. When a young boy vanishes, a small town uncovers a mystery involving secret experiments, terrifying supernatural forces and one strange little girl.

Created by Matt Duffer, Ross Duffer, Shawn Levy, Dan Cohen

Starring: Winona Ryder, David Harbour, Millie Bobby Brown, Finn Wolfhard, Gaten Matarazzo, Sadie Sink.
